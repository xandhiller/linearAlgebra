\[%
    \text{Rank(}\mathbf{A}) + \text{dim(Nul(}\mathbf{A})) = n
\]%

\[%
    \therefore n=8
\]%
and with there being two dependent equations:
\[%
   \text{dim(Nul(}\mathbf{A})) = 2
\]%
so
\[%
    \text{Rank(}\mathbf{A})  = n  - \text{dim(Nul(}\mathbf{A})) = 8 - 2 
\]%
\[%
    \therefore \text{Rank(}\mathbf{A}) = 6
